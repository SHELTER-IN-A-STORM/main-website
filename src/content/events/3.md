# GFWC Food Drive Success

**Date:** September 30, 2023  
**Time:** 9:00 AM - 2:00 PM  
**Location:** Lake Ozark Area

## Event Details

GFWC (General Federation of Women's Clubs) hosted a successful Food Drive and fundraising event benefiting "Shelter in a Storm". Through the incredible generosity of our community, this amazing group collected:

- $975 in monetary donations
- 160 food items

Their support helps us continue our vital mission of serving those in need in our community.

*Thank you to everyone who contributed to this successful community event!*
