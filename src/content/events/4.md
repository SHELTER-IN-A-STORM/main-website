# 2nd Annual Fall Fundraiser

**Date:** Saturday, November 1, 2025  
**Time:** 5:00 PM - 8:00 PM  
**Location:** Memory Lane Events, 78 Walnut Grove Road, Eldon, MO

![Fall Fundraiser 2025](https://cdn.shelterinastorm.org/images/2025-FallFundraiser-Front-1.png "Fall Fundraiser 2025")

## Event Details

Join us for our exciting Fall Fundraiser featuring dinner, bake-off competition, live music, and silent auction! All proceeds will benefit Shelter in a Storm.

## Event Schedule

- **5:00 PM** - Cash Bar Opens
- **6:00 PM** - Dinner & Bake-off Tasting
- **7:15 PM** - Bake-off Trophy Awarded

## What to Expect

- **Dinner** - Enjoy a delicious meal
- **Bake-off Competition** - Taste amazing baked goods and vote for your favorites
- **Live Music** - Enjoy "Me & Jim" pick their guitars to 60s & 70s music
- **Silent Auction** - Bid on unique items and experiences
- **Cash Bar** - Refreshments available
