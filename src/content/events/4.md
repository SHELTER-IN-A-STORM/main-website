# 2nd Annual Fall Fundraiser

**Date:** Saturday, November 1, 2025  
**Time:** 5:00 PM - 8:00 PM  
**Location:** Memory Lane Events, 78 Walnut Grove Road, Eldon, MO

![Fall Fundraiser 2025](https://cdn.shelterinastorm.org/images/2025-FallFundraiser-Front-1.png "Fall Fundraiser 2025")

## Event Recap

Our 100 guests enjoyed tasting and voting on the creative Bake-Off entries, listening to our live entertainment, "Me & Jim", and had competitive fun bidding on the auction items that were donated by 62 businesses and individuals in our community.

## Event Highlights

- **Bake-Off Competition** - Creative entries were tasted and voted on by our guests
- **Live Music** - "Me & Jim" provided entertainment picking their guitars to 60s & 70s music
- **Silent Auction** - Competitive bidding on items donated by 62 businesses and individuals
- **Dinner** - Delicious meal enjoyed by all attendees
- **Cash Bar** - Refreshments available throughout the evening
