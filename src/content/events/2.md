Thanks to our generous community, our 2023 Winter Coat Drive was a tremendous success! Together, we collected and distributed over 500 coats, scarves, gloves, and winter accessories to local families in need.

## Event Highlights
- Over 200 families received winter clothing assistance
- Local businesses donated hot chocolate and refreshments
- Volunteers helped sort and distribute items
- Prayer and support provided to all attendees

## Impact
- 500+ winter items collected and distributed
- 50+ volunteers participated
- 15 local businesses contributed
- 4 hours of continuous community service

We're grateful for everyone who contributed to making this event successful. Your donations helped ensure that our neighbors stayed warm during the cold winter months. Special thanks to our volunteers and local business partners who made this possible.
