Our Fall Fundraiser was a huge success! The evening was filled with amazing chili, laughter, and generous community support. 142 guests enjoyed tasting and voting on their favorite chili entries, were entertained by the very funny comedian, Bryce Stanley, and bid on over 40 items in our Silent Auction.

## Event Highlights

- Chili tasting and voting competition
- Comedy performance by Bryce Stanley  
- Silent auction featuring over 40 unique items
- Community gathering and fellowship
- Fundraising for local support initiatives

## Impact

Together we raised $7,200 - a testament to our community's commitment to helping those in need. These funds will directly support our mission to provide emergency assistance and resources to families in our area.

## Thank You

We extend our heartfelt gratitude to:
- All chili cook-off participants
- Our generous auction item donors

- Our dedicated volunteers
