# Community Chili Cook-Off

**Date:** January 20, 2024  
**Time:** 11:00 AM - 3:00 PM  
**Location:** Lake Ozark Christian Church, 1560 Bagnell Dam Blvd.

## Event Details

Join us for our annual Chili Cook-Off fundraiser benefiting our 24/7 emergency food pantry! Sample delicious chili from local cooks, vote for your favorite, and help support our neighbors in need.

### What's Included:
- Unlimited chili samples
- Cornbread and fixings
- Soft drinks and water
- 1 voting token for People's Choice Award
- Live music and entertainment

## Purchase Tickets

Tickets are $15 per person, children under 5 eat free. All proceeds support our emergency food assistance programs.

[Purchase Tickets](https://tickets.shelterinastorm.org/chili-2024)

Want to compete? Enter your chili for $25 - includes 2 event tickets! Contact us for competitor registration.
