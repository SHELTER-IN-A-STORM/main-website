Join us for our biggest community event of the year! The Community Food Drive & Family Fun Day brings together local families for a day of giving, entertainment, and community building. Help us stock our emergency food pantry while enjoying activities for all ages.

## Event Details
- Free admission with suggested donation of non-perishable food items
- Family-friendly activities including face painting and bounce houses
- Live local music performances throughout the day
- Food trucks and refreshments available

## Schedule
- 10:00 AM: Event opens - Begin food collection and family activities
- 12:00 PM: Special performance by local praise band
- 3:30 PM: Community prayer and closing ceremony

Your participation helps ensure our emergency food pantry remains stocked and ready to serve those in need throughout our Lake Ozark community. Together, we can make a difference in the lives of our neighbors.
