# Christmas Carol Outreach at Lake Ozark Village

**Date:** December 12, 2024  
**Time:** 9:00 AM - 2:00 PM  
**Location:** Lake Ozark Village

## Event Details

Join us as Lake Ozark Christian Church members visit Lake Ozark Village to spread Christmas cheer through caroling and gift-giving. We'll be delivering presents and singing traditional Christmas carols to bring joy to village residents during this special holiday season.

### What's Included:
- Christmas caroling by church members
- Gift delivery to village residents 
- Holiday treats and refreshments
- Spreading Christmas cheer through song and fellowship

## How to Participate

Church members who would like to participate in caroling and gift delivery are welcome to join. We'll meet at Lake Ozark Christian Church before heading to Lake Ozark Village together.

To donate gifts or join the caroling group, please contact us using the information below.
