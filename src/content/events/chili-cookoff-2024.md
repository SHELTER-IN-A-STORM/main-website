Get ready for our most anticipated event of the year - the Annual Chili Cook-Off! Whether you're a seasoned chef or just love great food, this event has something for everyone.

## Event Details
- Entry fee: $25 per chili submission
- Free admission for tasters with suggested donation
- Prizes for 1st, 2nd, and 3rd place winners
- People's Choice Award

## Schedule
- 5:00 PM: Contestant setup and chili submission
- 5:30 PM: Tasting begins
- 7:30 PM: Awards ceremony
- 8:00 PM: Event concludes

## What to Expect
- Sample amazing chili recipes from local cooks
- Vote for your favorite chili
- Live entertainment
- Family-friendly atmosphere
- Cornbread and dessert available for purchase

All proceeds support our food pantry program, helping provide emergency food assistance to families in need throughout Lake Ozark. Join us for an evening of great food, friendly competition, and community support!
